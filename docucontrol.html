What is DocuControl™

Document control that actually works

Summit Sync DocuControl™ is a purpose-built document management and control platform for organisations operating under ISO management systems. It replaces shared drives, email chains, and paper-based document registers with a structured, auditable, cloud-accessible system.

From the moment a document is created to the moment it's obsoleted, every step is logged, every version is tracked, and every acknowledgement is recorded. Audit-ready at all times.

DocuControl™ is built around the ISO document control requirements of ISO 9001, 14001, 45001, and 22301 — making it the ideal companion to Summit's consulting practice.

📋
Full document register
Every document with ID, type, version, owner, reviewer, approver, review date, status, and ISO clause — searchable and filterable.
🔄
Structured approval workflow
Draft → Review → Pending Approval → Approved → Effective. Every stage requires the right person's sign-off before progressing.
✍️
Read & acknowledge tracking
Assign acknowledgement requirements to any document and track who has and hasn't read it — by department or individual.
🔎
Full audit trail
Every action — upload, review, approval, acknowledgement — is logged with user, timestamp, and details. Always audit-ready.
🏷️
ISO clause tagging
Tag every document to the relevant ISO clause — so retrieving evidence for a surveillance audit takes seconds, not hours.

More Features

Built for ISO compliance

Every feature in DocuControl™ is designed around what ISO auditors look for — and what compliance teams need to manage day to day.

🔐
Role-based access control
Super Admin, Owner, and Viewer roles — each with precisely defined permissions. Viewers can read but never edit. Only authorised users can approve.
🏢
Multi-department support
Documents are assigned to departments, and access can be restricted by department. Everyone sees what they should — and nothing they shouldn't.
🔎
Global search
Search across all documents by ID, title, owner, department, or keyword — from the top bar on every screen. Find any document instantly.
📎
File upload (PDF, DOCX, XLSX & more)
Attach the actual document file to every record — PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, or image. Up to 50MB per file. Always accessible from the register.
🏷️
ISO clause tagging
Tag each document to ISO 9001, 14001, 45001, or 22301 clauses — enabling instant evidence retrieval for any external audit.
🗄️
Obsolete archive
Obsoleted documents are never deleted — they live in the Obsolete Archive as retained evidence of previous controls. Always accessible, never current.
