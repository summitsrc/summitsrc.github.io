Executive Overview

One gateway. Every Summit Sync application.

Summit Sync Workspace is the secure digital operating platform for the entire Summit Sync 360° ecosystem. It centralises authentication, subscription management, application delivery, user administration, software updates, support, and customer services into a single enterprise workspace.

Organisations use the Workspace as their gateway to every Summit Sync application — iCORE, SafeTrack, DocuControl, and CAPA Manager — while maintaining complete ownership of their operational data. The Workspace manages the platform. The applications manage the work.

Built on a cloud-managed, local-first architecture, Workspace ensures your compliance data stays in your control while platform services — licensing, updates, support — are handled seamlessly in the cloud.

The Workspace IS responsible for
Authentication & access control
Licensing & subscription management
User & organisation management
Software delivery & updates
Support ticket management
Notices & announcements
Downloads & release management
Knowledge base & training
The Workspace IS NOT responsible for
Incident management
Visitor management
Document storage
CAPA workflows
Training records
Investigation data
Product Principles

Built on eight principles

These principles are not aspirational — they are the design rules that govern every decision made in building Summit Sync Workspace.

☁️
Cloud-managed
Platform services — licensing, updates, support, user management — are managed in the cloud with zero on-premise infrastructure required.
🖥️
Local-first operational data
Operational data — incidents, musters, documents — stays in your application environment. The Workspace never touches it.
🧩
Modular architecture
Add or remove Summit Sync applications independently. Each module is self-contained but shares the Workspace authentication layer.
🔒
Secure by default
MFA, session timeout, audit logging, and device approval are on by default — not optional add-ons.
📈
Enterprise scalable
From 25-user Starter plans to unlimited-user Enterprise deployments — Workspace scales without architectural changes.
🏢
Organisation isolated
Every client's data, users, and devices are completely isolated. No cross-tenant data access is architecturally possible.
📶
Offline-capable applications
Summit Sync applications continue operating during connectivity loss. Workspace syncs when connection is restored.
✨
Simple customer experience
One login. One portal. Every licence, download, support ticket, and notice in a single, clean interface.
