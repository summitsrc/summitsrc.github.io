What is Summit WorkFlow

Every task. Every team. One system.

Summit WorkFlow is a purpose-built work order management platform designed for organisations that need to assign, track, approve, and close tasks — across maintenance, safety, operations, facilities, IT, and more.

From the moment a work order is created to the moment it's closed and approved, every action is logged, every assignee is notified, and every manager has full visibility — no spreadsheets, no WhatsApp chains, no dropped tasks.

Built for teams that run sites. Whether you're managing a port, a manufacturing plant, or a multi-site operation, WorkFlow gives supervisors and managers the visibility they need and field teams the clarity they deserve.

📋
Structured work orders
Every task has a number, category, department, assignee, due date, checklist, and status — nothing gets lost or forgotten.
✅
Built-in approval workflow
Work orders that require approval flow from Completed → Waiting on Approval → Approved without manual chasing.
📌
Kanban board view
Drag and drop work orders across status columns for a live visual of everything in flight across your team.
📐
Reusable templates
Create templates for recurring tasks — weekly inspections, monthly maintenance, standard procedures — and launch them in seconds.
📊
Real-time reports
Workload by assignee, overdue tasks, completion rates, time tracking, and department performance — exportable to PDF and Excel.

Priority Levels

Five priority levels. Always visible.

Every work order carries a priority — colour-coded across every view so urgent work is never buried.

🔴
Critical
Immediate action required. Security risk, safety hazard, or operational stoppage.
🟠
High
Significant impact if not resolved promptly. Same-day or next-day attention required.
🟡
Medium
Important but not urgent. Should be completed within the week.
🔵
Low
Can wait. Schedule when capacity allows.
⚪
Planning
Not yet scheduled. In planning or awaiting resource allocation.
