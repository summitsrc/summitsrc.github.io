Features

Everything your site needs

Every feature is included in every plan. Nothing locked behind a higher tier.

🚧
Gate contractor check-in
Log contractors as they arrive, assign muster zones, track supervisors on site. Check-outs remove them from the live count automatically.
📱
Zone muster tablets
Dedicated tablet views for Zone A and Zone B wardens. Tap a card to mark someone safe — command center updates instantly.
🖥️
Live command center
Real-time dashboard showing total on site, confirmed safe, and unaccounted — split by zone with contractor and warden tagging.
🪪
Visitor management
Log visitors separately from employees. Track purpose, host, and area visited. Visitors are excluded from muster headcount but visible throughout.
📋
Instant drill reports
Close the drill and generate a PDF report with full zone breakdowns, timestamps, drill duration, and unaccounted personnel list.
⏱️
Live drill timer
A visible timer from the moment the drill starts, with a pulsing live indicator so every warden knows the clock is running.
📊
Swipe file import
Accepts your existing .xlsx, .xls, or .csv timekeeping exports. No reformatting required — SafeTrack detects zones automatically.
⭐
Warden & ERT tagging
Wardens and ERT members are automatically tagged from your upload, making them instantly visible during any emergency.
🌐
Browser-based, no installs
Runs on any device with a browser — phones, tablets, laptops. No app downloads, no IT setup, no hardware required.
