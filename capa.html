What is CAPA Manager™

Every action. From source to closed.

Summit Sync CAPA Manager™ is a full corrective and preventive action tracking platform for organisations that need to manage findings, nonconformances, and improvement actions across multiple departments and business units.

From the moment an action is raised — whether from an audit finding, incident, inspection, customer complaint, or management review — to the moment evidence is uploaded and closure is verified, every step is tracked, timestamped, and audit-ready.

Built around the PDCA cycle (Plan, Do, Check, Act), CAPA Manager™ ensures corrective actions are not just raised — they are completed, evidenced, and verified effective before being closed.

📋
Centralised action register
Every CAPA in one place — searchable, filterable by status, priority, department, source, or business unit. No spreadsheets, no lost actions.
📎
Evidence upload & tracking
Attach photos, documents, and reports directly to each action as closure evidence. Actions can't close without verified evidence.
⬆️
Report upload & extraction
Upload audit or inspection reports and extract action items directly — no manual re-keying of findings from PDF reports.
🔔
Automated reminders
Action owners receive automatic reminders before due dates. Overdue actions are flagged prominently — the system chases for you.
📊
Live analytics & charts
Six live charts showing actions by department, status, owner, source type, business unit, and high-risk items — updated in real time.
Built on PDCA

The improvement cycle built in.

Every CAPA in the system follows the Plan-Do-Check-Act cycle. Not just a philosophy — a structured workflow that ensures corrective actions actually drive improvement.

P
Plan
Raise the action. Identify the root cause, assign an owner, set a due date, define the corrective action required, and classify the risk level and priority.
D
Do
The assignee implements the corrective action. Progress is tracked in the register. Evidence files — photos, reports, documents — are uploaded as work is completed.
C
Check
The action moves to Pending Closure. A verifier reviews the evidence, confirms effectiveness, and either approves closure or returns the action for further work.
A
Act
Once verified, the action is closed with a full audit trail — what was found, what was done, who verified it, and when. Lessons are available for future reference.

More Features

Built for real compliance teams

Every feature is designed around how QHSE compliance actually works — not how software vendors think it does.

🔔
Automated reminder log
Every reminder sent — to every action owner, for every action — is logged with status (sent, failed, pending). Full reminder audit trail always available.
🏢
Multi-business unit
Switch between business units from the sidebar. Each BU has its own register view — admins see all units simultaneously for cross-organisation reporting.
🗑️
Deleted actions archive
Deleted actions are never permanently lost — they move to the Deleted Actions screen, visible to admins for recovery or audit evidence.
⏸️
Deferral workflow
Actions can be formally deferred with a reason and new due date — so legitimate delays are documented, not hidden, and don't distort overdue counts.
🔐
Role-based access
Admin users see everything and can manage all settings. Viewer users see the register but cannot create, edit, or close actions.
🕐
Status stepper
Every action detail view shows a visual status stepper — Open → In Progress → Pending Closure → Closed — so progress is visible at a glance.
