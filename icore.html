Module 1 — Incident Management
Full incident lifecycle — report to close
Log, investigate, analyse, and close incidents with structured root cause analysis and automatic notifications to the right people.
📝
Incident register
Full register with type, severity, location, department, reporter, and lifecycle status — filterable and searchable.
🔔
Auto-notifications
Supervisors notified on all incidents. Safety managers and executives automatically alerted for critical severity events.
🔍
Root cause analysis tools
Built-in RCA panels: 5 Whys, Fishbone Diagram, Barrier Analysis, and Human Factors — all within the incident record.
🔗
Direct CAPA creation
Create a corrective action directly from the incident record — no re-keying, all context carried across.
📎
Evidence attachment
Attach photos, documents, and investigation notes directly to the incident record for a complete audit trail.

Module 2 — Corrective Action Tracker
Every action tracked from source to closure
A centralised CAPA register pulling from incidents, audits, management reviews, and customer complaints — with a clear workflow from open to verified closed.
📋
Centralised CAPA register
All corrective actions in one place regardless of source — incidents, audits, management reviews, or customer complaints.
🔄
5-stage workflow
Open → Assigned → In Progress → Awaiting Verification → Closed. Every status change is timestamped and auditable.
🚨
Overdue highlighting
Overdue actions are flagged in red across the register and dashboard. Automatic reminders keep assignees accountable.
✅
Effectiveness verification
Assignee marks complete, verifier reviews evidence before closing. No action closes without verified effectiveness.
👤
Full assignment fields
Priority, department, due date, assignee, verifier, and description — every field needed to drive accountability.

Module 3 — Document Control
Controlled documents, always current
A controlled document register with version control, approval workflows, ISO standard tagging, and per-department acknowledgement tracking.
📄
Controlled document register
Every document with version number, review date, approval status, department, and ISO standard — searchable and filterable.
✍️
Approval workflow
Draft → Under Review → Approved → Controlled → Archived. Documents require approval before becoming controlled.
👁️
Read & acknowledge tracking
Per-department acknowledgement progress bars. See exactly who has and hasn't acknowledged each document.
🏷️
ISO standard tagging
Tag documents to ISO 9001, 14001, 45001, or 22301 clauses — making audit evidence retrieval instant.
📤
Upload with drag & drop
Upload modal with drag-and-drop, document type selection, approver assignment, and save as draft or submit for review.

Platform Features

Built for the way teams actually work

Every feature in iCORE is designed to reduce friction, drive accountability, and make compliance feel less like admin.

🌙
Dark mode
Full dark mode toggle from the sidebar. Designed for control room environments and late-night incident reporting without eye strain.
📱
Fully responsive
Responsive grid layouts throughout — iCORE works on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Report an incident from the field, not just the office.
🔍
Universal search
Search across incidents, actions, and documents from a single search bar. Find any record instantly without navigating between modules.
🧭
Collapsible sidebar
A clean collapsible sidebar with live badge counts showing open items per module — so you always know what needs attention.
🔔
Toast notifications
Real-time toast notifications for every action — incident reported, action assigned, document approved. No page refresh needed.
🏢
Role & org indicator
Your role, organisation, and applicable ISO standards are displayed in the top bar — so every user knows their compliance context.
