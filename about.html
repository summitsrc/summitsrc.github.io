Our Story

Built for the Caribbean

Summit Safety, Risk & Compliance Authority was founded with a clear purpose — to give Caribbean organisations access to the same world-class safety, risk, and compliance expertise that larger international operations take for granted.

Too often, local businesses face global regulatory requirements without the local support to meet them. Certification bodies, international standards, and compliance frameworks are designed for contexts far removed from the realities of operating in The Bahamas and the wider Caribbean region.

Summit changes that. We bring ISO-certified expertise, practical management system design, and purpose-built technology directly to the organisations that need it most — ports, airports, utilities, manufacturers, government agencies, and every high-stakes operation in between.

Our team combines over 40 years of combined experience in ISO management systems, occupational health and safety, risk management, business continuity, and digital compliance — all applied with deep knowledge of the Caribbean operating environment.
